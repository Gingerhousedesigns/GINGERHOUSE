COMPANY

GINGERHOUSE LLC

The Gingerhouse is an Idea Development Company. With experience in architecture, construction, and fabrication, I am able to take an idea from concept to reality. Services include Product Design, Prototyping, Scale Modeling, Design Documentation, and General Contracting. Contact me for a quote on your next project.

CREATOR

Evan Hammans

After graduating from the University of Cincinnati with a Bachelors of Science in Architecture degree, I moved to NY to practice as an Architectural Designer. I moved back to Indianapolis to start the Gingerhouse LLC. My passion for innovation and collaberation has drove me to livestream, everyday, the work I do at the Gingerhouse. This lets me document all of my progress and get instant feedback from anyone. Work can be fun and I'm out to prove it. Thanks for stopping by the Gingerhouse.
